Completed the Missing numbers problem.
Used a loop to calculate the sum of all given numbers and the formula n*(n+1)/2 to calculate the Expected/Actual Sum, and then performed the Expected Sum - Obtained Sum to get the result, i.e. the missing number.
The algorithm initially took a time of 0ms and 21.82 MB of space, but with a few optimisations, it was brought down to 0ms and 21.76 MB.
The time and space complexity of the algorithm are O(n) and O(1), respectively.
